Summer Collabs are here

Collaborations seem to always bring much needed energy to things and this week we've got some good ones releasing this week.


So, while we anxiously await 😴 the rumored rematch between Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao, and simultaneously try to make sense of the Kawhi Leonard for Demar DeRozan and Danny Green deal, let's get right into some of the new releases.

Vogue x Air Jordan 1

Style icon and infamous Editor-In-Chief of Vogue, Anna Wintour, gave her stamp of approval on this collab. Bids are well above retail going into the official release date.

Parra Is Back With New Air Max Drops 🎨

The Dutch artist Parra is, arguably responsible for some of the most sought after Air Max designs of all time. He's back with a new trio of new releases that are already selling for 2-3 times retail. The Friends and Family has Bids waiting as high as $2500! Have you ever seen an Air Max 1 without a Swoosh?

Pure White 3s For Below Retail

The newest retro Jordan lands this week in the form of the Pure White Jordan 3. The icy blue outsole on this summer-ready colorway adds just enough to make it subtle.
